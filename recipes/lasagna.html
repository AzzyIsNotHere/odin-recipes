Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is sure to blow your socks off, with ricotta cheese, a homemade

meat sauce, and enough saucy, cheesey goodness to satisfy you and your family!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 pound of sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 minced clove of garlic
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3 teaspoons of salt, divided
  • 3 teaspoons of Italian seasoning, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of minced fresh flat-leaf parsley, divided
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 15oz. whole milk Ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 large egg
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Make the meat sauce: Add beef and sausage to a large skillet, over medium-high heat whilst breaking it apart with a wooden spoon. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until the meat is browned, about seven minutes. Then, drain the fat from the meat and return the meat to the skillet.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 2 teaspoons of salt, 2 teaspoons of Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon of basil, and the black pepper. Stir until well-combined. Cover and reduce the heat to low. Simmer the sauce for 30 minutes. Stir in 2 tablespoons of the minced parsley.
  4. As the sauce cooks, make the ricotta mixture. In a large bowl, add 1 cup mozzarella, the ricotta, ¼ cup parmesan, 2 tablespoons of the parsley, the egg, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, and ½ teaspoon basil. Stir until well incorporated.
  5. Cook the pasta al dente, according to it’s package directions.
  6. Assemble the lasagna. In a 13x9 inch baking dish, add a very thin layer of meat sauce (about ¼ cup). Layer 3 lasagna noodles, ⅓ of the ricotta mixture, and 1 ½ (level) cups of meat sauce. Repeat the layers twice.
  7. Add a final layer of the remaining noodles, meat sauce, 1 cup mozzarella, and ¼ cup parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil that has been sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil. Bake 15 more minutes.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 15-20 minutes before cutting.
